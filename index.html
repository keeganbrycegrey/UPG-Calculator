Formula & methodology
UPG = 2.8101 − 0.047147·ZMA − 0.046402·ZRC − 0.1381·ZLP
     − 0.15531·ZHSWA − 0.025178·ZSC·ZLP·ZHSWA
UPCAT subtest Z-scores: (raw score − mean) ÷ SD, using estimated population params (see advanced).
HSWA standardization: ZHSWA = (HSWA − 85) ÷ 5
HSWA = Final grades in Grades 8, 9, 10, and 11.
EEAS: palugit = −0.05 · pabigat = +0.05.
DISCLAIMER: ⚠ UP internally transmutes HS grades (160+ school systems) - your self-reported HSWA may differ from what UP uses for its calculations.
